Hi, I'm Aleksandur also known as AJ. I am a creative technologies student at Illinois State University and I’m planning on graduating in the spring of 2024. I’ve always enjoyed drawing and being creative ever since I was a kid when my mom bought me a Super Mario how to draw book. I would draw and cut out the little characters and mail them to my family that I couldn’t see often. I took a break from drawing in high school to focus on graphic design and video editing but I’m finally back and ready to please my inner child. Hopefully, you enjoy what you see on this site and I’m excited to post more in the future!
